Your workday

Documentation that fits into the day

Eleos helps turn live sessions or short clinical summaries into structured draft notes, so documentation fits more naturally into the workday and teams can keep their focus on delivering high‑quality, person‑centered care.

Some agencies report ≈70% less time spent on documentation.
Client impact

Evidence of stronger outcomes

Studies of Eleos‑enabled care show higher client engagement and larger reductions in symptoms compared to treatment as usual, including more visits attended and greater improvement in depression and anxiety.

67% more sessions attended in one randomized trial.

Supporting you while you support others

Community organizations using Eleos describe a more sustainable rhythm to their work: documentation expectations are easier to meet, teams can stay centered on the individuals they serve, and leadership can align schedules and supports around staff well‑being.
